about

one of my biggest pastimes is listening to music; my favorite genres are rock, r&b, jazz, and any combination of the former. i especially like a japanese artist named ringo sheena, along with her band tokyo incidents. it's hard (in a good way) to put a name or genre to what she does, but i have seen her compared to bjork, who she has said she is inspired by. i picked out a few of my personal favorites from her & her band below, with music videos if i could find them. the songs i chose lean towards jazz/rock/orchestral, but there is more electronic/"experimental" music too ( eg. box of goldfish). "camouflage" and "a distress" are some of my absolute favorites.
